The Session

Your session may consist of a few outfit changes, but we would request you to bring at least 6 to 8 different items of clothing with you. You must try to make the most of your session by bringing a variety of clothes with different styles and colours. Please ensure these are washed and ironed. This may include Formal, Evening and Smart Casual, Dresses, Skirts, Tops, Shirts, Jackets, Suits, Swimwear, Lingerie, Dance and Beach Wear. Bring accessories such as belts, overcoats, jewellery, wigs, shoes, tights and matching undergarments.

If you have any Skin Allergies, please inform us on your arrival at the Studio. The Studio takes no responsibility in case of an allergic reaction.

Photographs

After the session, you will be asked by one of our viewing consultants to view and select the best images from your session for printing. Extra prints can be ordered by paying a 50% deposit, which cannot be cancelled. Full balance must be paid before collecting your prints.

Prints must be collected within 3 weeks. If these are not collected, the Studio may destroy and delete all prints and images after the 3 months period. Once you leave the Studio with your prints, any changes or refunds are not permitted, either in partial or in full.

Payments & Collection

We do not accept Cheques or Amex cards. You can pay by Cash or by Debit/Credit cards. There is a 10% charge on all Card payments, bank transfers and online payments. Photographs ordered for printing may take up to 20 working days to be ready for collection. Once ready, a text will be sent to notify you.
